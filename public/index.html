The hobby I want to learn.

I have many hobbies including playing guitar, sports and outdoor activities. However, there is a hobby that I am really interested in and cannot wait to try. That is kite boarding!

Kite boarding involves strapping yourself to a giant kite, jumping in the ocean and riding a board. While kite boarding, it is possible to get several feet in the air while riding waves. Fortunately, Saint Petersburg is a hub for kite boarding; people travel from all over the world to kite board here in St Pete. During the winter months when windy days are plentiful, one can find several people by the Skyway Bridge practicing their kite boarding skills. I intend to take lessons as soon as winter comes back around!
